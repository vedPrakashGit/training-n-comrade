Our Story

Training & Coaching Comrade, a pan India based organization operating in online and offline mode, delivering short term courses and catering to the needs and aspirations of millennial youth looking to grow in the next gen corporate ecosystem. India being the youngest and swiftly developing nation, with its 65% population falling in the youth category i.e.; under the age of 35 has landed with immense opportunities as India is set to be the fastest growing large economy in the world. We as a training organization skill the aforesaid young population to fulfill the need of the growing ecosystem through skilling the educated and underprivileged youth meeting the demands of several growing industries.

From The Founder

I am essentially a south Indian from the state, Kerala. Born and brought up in Delhi with a decent schooling from Kendriya Vidhyalaya and graduated from a Delhi University college, Ram Lal Anand in south campus with a bachelor’s degree in History. As a great admirer of the McDonald’s, my mind was set to start my career from the same and so, as I completed my graduation I joined without much difficulty and kick started my professional life. I am recently married and currently living in Yamunanagar, Haryana which is also the place where my organization was founded.

Started off my career as a crew member at McDonald’s and got an early promotion as a crew trainer or, as we call it in the training industry, a process trainer. The speedy advancement within the system wasn’t a surprise nor very challenging, graced by decent education; but surely an experience that brought me face to face with a certain skill that I was unaware of and couldn’t give a name to. This innate quality was noticed by one of my current mentors who thankfully picked me from the place and introduced me to the training industry.

From there on I worked with big and small organizations and grew from a soft skills trainer providing training to the underprivileged through premier government projects (DDU-GKY, PMKVY) that were taken up by private organizations to becoming an integral part of startups as a business development manager where I would hire people, build teams, lead and direct for organizational goals. As I was progressing in my career, I was also drifting away from the trainer within me. That’s precisely the time where I took a pause from my career and planned to get myself acquainted with the progressive training styles and find my niche in the process.

I am now an internationally certified trainer and coach certified by the Indian Leadership Academy and also the founder of Training and Coaching Comrade that aims to bring a change within the society in small yet significant ways as well as providing opportunity to the underprivileged to enter the mainstream and escalate in life with more than 5 years of experience in the industry.
