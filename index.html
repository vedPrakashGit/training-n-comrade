About Us

Training & Coaching Comrade, a pan India based organization operating in online and offline mode, delivering short term courses and catering to the needs and aspirations of millennial youth looking to grow in the next gen corporate ecosystem. India being the youngest and swiftly developing nation, with its 65% population falling in the youth category i.e.; under the age of 35 has landed with immense opportunities as India is set to be the fastest growing large economy in the world. We as a training organization skill the aforesaid young population to fulfill the need of the growing ecosystem through skilling the educated and underprivileged youth meeting the demands of several growing industries.

Meet Our Founder

Priyanka Prabhakaran Dhingra is an internationally certified Trainer and Coach, certified by the Indian Leadership Academy and the founder of Training and Coaching Comrade, with more than 5 years of experience in the industry along with being a certified menstrual educator by Menstrupedia.


From providing training to the underprivileged through premier government projects (DDU-GKY, PMKVY) to becoming an integral part of startups as a business development manager, she now aims to bring a change within the society in small yet significant ways as well as providing opportunity to the underprivileged to enter the mainstream and escalate in life.

Our Workshops

Menstrual Education

View Details

The problem of menstrual hygiene is multifaceted; girls need to be aware about menarche and be able to manage their menstruation in an enabling environment with access to hygienic menstrual materials and facilities.
Menstruation is celebrated in most parts of India and yet cultural taboos exist which regularly limit girls from activities during menstruation, including religious restrictions, and freedom to leave the house. This contributes to negative attitudes toward menstruation among women, placing a considerable physical and psychological burden on young girls.
The workshop aims to create awareness around all aforementioned issues and empower women to progress in lives.

Topics
  • Ice-breakers
  • Psychology of Menstruation
  • Hygiene during periods
  • Growing Up
  • Preparing for Periods
  • Menstrual Disorders
  • Nutrition
